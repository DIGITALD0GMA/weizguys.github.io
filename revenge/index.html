Sweethearts Revenge Homebrew Competition

The Sweethearts Revenge Homebrew Competition is held in February in Loveland, Colorado.

Special Category for 2019: Valentine's Brew

We will have a special category with a special award. This year’s special category is Valentine’s Day themed brews. What that means is up to the brewer. Is that a pink glitter beer? A rose flavored mead or cider? A spicy beer for a fiery romance? Check official rules and regulations for additional information.

Awards

Awards will be given to the top entries in each category. Entries must have a minimum score of 30 points to be eligible for a medal. Categories may be combined at the discretion of the competition organizer to ensure an adequate judging pool.

Entrants do not need to be present to win an award. If you are unable to attend, your award will be shipped to the address provided in your ReggieBeer registration.
