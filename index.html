Welcome to the WeizGuys

Welcome to the Weiz Guys Homebrewing Club of Loveland, CO. The Weiz Guys club was formed in 1996 and still going strong.

We meet the 3rd Tuesday of every month at 7:00pm. We meet at different breweries each month. Please check the calendar below for the next meeting's location. Our meetings are open to everyone (21 years old or older) interested in sharing beer and beer knowledge. Our meetings are fun and relaxed. Come join us and feel free to bring your homebrew to share.
